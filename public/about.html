About Our Academy

Building futures through quality education since 2018

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Student Academy Portal was created with a single mission: to make quality education accessible to everyone. We believe that the right training and mentorship can transform careers and lives.

Our Journey

  • 2018: Academy founded with first cohort of 50 students
  • 2019: Expanded curriculum; reached 500+ students
  • 2020: Launched online platform during pandemic
  • 2021: 85% employment rate; industry partnerships
  • 2022: Mobile app launch; 1000+ graduates
  • 2023: International scholarships program
  • 2024: 2000+ active students, 50+ hiring partners
  • 2025: Achieved 4.8/5 student satisfaction rating
